Poem of the Day 每日诗歌

Interno
Sandro Penna

Dal portiere non c’era nessuno.
C’era la luce sui poveri letti
disfatti. E sopra un tavolaccio
dormiva un ragazzaccio
bellissimo. Uscì dalle sue braccia
annuvolate, esitando, un gattino.

Interior
Sandro Penna

There was no one at the reception
There was light on the poor beds
unmade. And on top of a bad table
was sleeping a young boy,
so beautiful. Out of his clouded arms
came, hesitating, a kitten.
