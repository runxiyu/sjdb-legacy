Poem of the Day 每日诗歌

مرغی دیدم نشسته بر باره‌ٔ توس
‮1در پیش نهاده کله‌ٔ کیکاوس
با کله همی‌گفت افسوس افسوس
کو بانگ جرسها و چه شد نالهٔ کوس

'I saw a bird perched on the walls of Tus
Holding before it Kei Kavus's1 skull;
And to this skull it cried, 'Alas! Alas!
Where now the sound of bells, the roll of drums?'

余见一鸟栖于图什之墙
其前所立凯卡乌斯1之颅
其连连叹息：呜呼！呜呼！
其钟鼓之声都已沉寂乎？

1 (from sharer) Key Kaaus, Key Kavus (usually "Kay"), was the mythical king and son of Key-Qobad.

On This Day 以史为鉴

April 18
4月18日：津巴布韦独立日（1980年）

  • 1775 – American Revolutionary War: Colonists Paul Revere and William Dawes, later joined by Samuel Prescott, began a midnight ride to warn residents of Lexington and Concord, Massachusetts, about the impending arrival of British troops.
  • 1938 – Superman, created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster of DC Comics, made his debut in Action Comics #1, the first true superhero comic book.
  • 1946 – The final session of the League of Nations concluded in Geneva, with delegates agreeing to transfer much of its assets to the United Nations.
  • 1980 – Robert Mugabe became the first prime minister of Zimbabwe, beginning a 37-year period in power.
  • 2007 – A ladle spilled 30 tonnes (33 tons) of molten steel in a factory in Liaoning, China, killing 32 workers.
  • 1025年 – 波兰公爵波列斯瓦夫一世在格涅兹诺加冕成为首位波兰国王，正式建立波兰王国。
  • 1506年 – 天主教会圣座在梵蒂冈开始兴建圣伯多禄大殿，取代4世纪时建造的旧圣伯多禄大殿。
  • 1906年 – 美国加利福尼亚州旧金山发生ML 7.9级强烈地震并引发多处大火，造成至少3,000人死亡。
  • 1946年 – 国际联盟在瑞士日内瓦召开最后一次会议后自行解散，其权力移交给新成立的联合国。
  • 1955年 – 印度尼西亚万隆召开万隆会议，共有29个亚洲和非洲第三世界国家和地区代表团参加。

Births and Deaths: Theobald of Bec (d. 1161); Clara Elsene Peck (b. 1883); Universo 2000 (b. 1963)
