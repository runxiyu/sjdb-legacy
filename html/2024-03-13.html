On This Day 以史为鉴

March 13
3月13日

  • 1781 – William Herschel discovered the planet Uranus from the garden of his house in Bath, England, initially considering it to be a comet.
  • 1845 – German composer Felix Mendelssohn's Violin Concerto was performed for the first time.
  • 1943 – The Holocaust: Nazi troops began the final liquidation of the Kraków Ghetto in German occupied Poland, sending about 2,000 Jews to the Płaszów labor camp, with the remaining 5,000 either killed or sent to Auschwitz.
  • 1988 – The Seikan Tunnel, 53.85 km (33.46 mi) in length, opened between the cities of Hakodate and Aomori, Japan.
  • 2013 – Cardinal Jorge Mario Bergoglio was elected Pope Francis, making him the first Jesuit pope, the first from the Americas, and the first from the Southern Hemisphere.
  • 1781年 – 英国业余天文学家威廉·赫歇尔在巴斯的住宅庭院内，使用自制的望远镜发现天王星。
  • 1881年 – 俄罗斯帝国沙皇亚历山大二世在圣彼得堡冬宫附近遭遇恐怖组织民意党成员刺杀身亡。
  • 1943年 – 纳粹德国党卫队进入位在波兰克拉科夫的犹太人区，将犹太人送往集中营强制劳动或杀害。
  • 1954年 – 由武元甲领导的越南独立同盟会军队向奠边府市的法国军队发动进攻，奠边府战役爆发。
  • 1988年 – 连接日本本州青森市与北海道函馆市的青函隧道正式通车，为当时世界最长的海底隧道。

Births and Deaths: Daniel Lambert (b. 1770); Mustafa Reşid Pasha (b. 1800); Helen Renton (b. 1931)
