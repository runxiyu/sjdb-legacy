On This Day 以史为鉴

December 6: Saint Nicholas Day (Western Christianity); Independence Day in Finland (1917)
12月6日：圣尼古拉节（西方基督教）、芬兰独立日（1917年）、西班牙宪法日（1978年）

  • 963 – Leo VIII was ordained a bishop, claiming the Holy See as an antipope supported by Otto the Great.
  • 1803 – Haitian Revolution: Nearly all the final French ships in Haiti were captured by the Royal Navy when they attempted to evade the blockade of Saint-Domingue.
  • 1907 – A mine explosion in Monongah, West Virginia, killed 362 people and led to the establishment of the United States Bureau of Mines.
  • 1941 – The British Secret Intelligence Service established a facility known as Camp X in Ontario, Canada, to train covert agents in clandestine operations.
  • 2015 – In the Venezuelan parliamentary election, the ruling United Socialist Party lost control of the National Assembly for the first time in 16 years.
  • 1865年 – 美国宪法第十三条修正案获得批准生效，正式废除奴隶制度。
  • 1905年 – 中国清朝政府奉旨设立学部，并决定自次年起废止延续千年的科举制度。
  • 1921年 – 英国与爱尔兰代表签订《英爱条约》，将爱尔兰岛划分为爱尔兰自由邦与北爱尔兰。
  • 1953年 – 俄罗斯裔美国作家弗拉基米尔·纳博科夫经过5年完成其争议小说《洛丽塔》。
  • 1956年 – 苏联与匈牙利于奥运水球半决赛爆发严重肢体冲突，最终由匈牙利以4比0获胜。

Births and Deaths: Maria de Dominici (b. 1645); Guillaume-Chrétien de Lamoignon de Malesherbes (b. 1721); Pete Rozelle (d. 1996)
