  • 1924 – Ramsay MacDonald took office as the first prime minister of the United Kingdom from the Labour Party.
  • 1943 – World War II: The Battle of Buna–Gona on New Guinea ended with an Allied victory after two months of difficult fighting against the well-prepared Japanese defences.
  • 1968 – Apollo 5, the first flight of NASA's Lunar Module, lifted off from Cape Kennedy Air Force Station.
  • 1979 – Uganda–Tanzania War: After surrounding the town of Mutukula, Uganda, the previous day, Tanzanian forces attacked the town in the Battle of Mutukula, causing the Ugandan forces to flee.

  • 1905年 – 俄罗斯军队在圣彼得堡的冬宫广场开枪镇压请愿工人，第一次俄国革命爆发。
