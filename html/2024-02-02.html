On This Day 以史为鉴

February 2: Candlemas (Western Christianity); Little New Year in northern China (2024); Groundhog Day in Canada and the United States
2月2日：世界湿地日、美国和加拿大土拨鼠日、日本双马尾日

  • 506 – Alaric II, King of the Visigoths, promulgated a collection of Roman law known as the Breviary of Alaric.
  • 1659 – Jan van Riebeeck, the founder of Cape Town, produced the first bottle of South African wine.
  • 1913 – New York City's Grand Central Terminal, the world's largest train station by number of platforms, opened immediately after midnight.
  • 1942 – The first act of the Norwegian resistance movement took place with the bombing of Oslo East Station by the Osvald Group to protest the inauguration of Vidkun Quisling.
  • 2009 – Omid, Iran's first domestically made satellite, was successfully launched from Semnan Space Center.
  • 962年 – 教宗若望十二世加冕奥托一世成为神圣罗马皇帝，建立神圣罗马帝国。
  • 1536年 – 西班牙征服者佩卓·门多萨率领远征队在南美洲建立布宜诺斯艾利斯。
  • 1709年 – 英国船长伍兹·罗杰斯及船员们营救被困在无人岛上的水手亚历山大·塞尔科克。
  • 1848年 – 美国与墨西哥签署转让土地的《瓜达卢佩-伊达尔戈条约》，美墨战争结束。
  • 1943年 – 德国元帅弗里德里希·保卢斯的第6军团向苏联红军投降，斯大林格勒战役结束。

Births and Deaths: Alix Le Clerc (b. 1576); José Guadalupe Posada (b. 1852); Alfred Delp (d. 1945); Peter Lundgren (b. 1963)
