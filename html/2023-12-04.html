Eye-sight and Refraction Exam 视力和屈光检查

  • Time: December 5th, all day
  • Locations: Wellness Center / Brain
  • Appointment Cards:
    • Boarders: receive your appointment cards from your HMs on Monday
    • Day students: take your appointment cards from the guards after school when you leave campus on Monday
  • Directions:
    • Go to class for attendance
    • Inform your teacher you need to leave for the eye exam (show teacher your appointment card if needed)
    • Look on appointment card to determine your location (Wellness Center / Brain)
    • Wait quietly at the location so we can get through these as fast as possible
    • Once done go back to class
    • Make sure to talk to the teacher or other classmates to catch up on what you have missed
  • Remember:
    • Please remain quiet
    • Please don't wear contact lenses
    • For nearsighted students, please wear frame glasses
    • Students wearing the Ortho-K at night should inform the wellness staff before the check
    • Contact Brittany Zhang for any questions
  • 时间：12 月 5 日全天
  • 地点：医务室 / 教研中心
  • 预约卡：
    • 住宿生：周一的时候从舍监那里领取预约卡
    • 走读生：在周一离校的时候到门卫那里领取预约卡
  • 说明：
    • 先去班级签到
    • 通知老师你需要离开去做视力检查（如需要，出示你的预约卡）
    • 查看预约卡来确定你进行视力检查的位置（医务室 / 教研中心）
    • 到达指定检查地点后请安静等待，以便我们能尽快完成检查
    • 完成检查后请立即回到上课班级
    • 一定要和老师或班上其他同学交流，以便赶上你错过的内容
  • 注意：
    • 请全程保持安静
    • 视力检查当天请不要佩戴隐形眼镜
    • 近视的同学请戴框架眼镜来检查视力
    • 晚上佩戴 OK 镜的同学请在检查视力之前告知工作人员
    • 有问题请联系 Brittany Zhang

Poem of the Day 每日诗歌

Mother to Son
Langston Hughes

Well, son, I'll tell you:
Life for me ain't been no crystal stair.
It's had tacks in it,
And splinters,
And boards torn up,
And places with no carpet on the floor—
Bare.
But all the time
I'se been a-climbin' on,
And reachin' landin's,
And turnin' corners,
And sometimes goin' in the dark
Where there ain't been no light.
So boy, don't you turn back.
Don't you set down on the steps
'Cause you finds it's kinder hard.
Don't you fall now—
For I'se still goin', honey,
I'se still climbin',
And life for me ain't been no crystal stair.
