On This Day 以史为鉴

January 11: Prithvi Jayanti in Nepal
1月11日：儿童节（突尼斯）

  • 1654 – Arauco War: The Mapuche-Huilliche of southern Chile defeated a slave-hunting Spanish army at the Battle of Río Bueno.
  • 1693 – The most powerful earthquake in Italian history, registering 7.4 Mw, struck the island of Sicily.
  • 1914 – The Karluk, flagship of the Canadian Arctic Expedition, sank after being crushed by ice.
  • 1964 – In a landmark report, U.S. Surgeon General Luther Terry issued the warning that smoking may be hazardous to people's health, concluding that it has a causative role in lung cancer, chronic bronchitis, and other illnesses.
  • 2003 – After Chicago police detective Jon Burge was discovered to have forced confessions from more than 200 suspects, the governor of Illinois commuted the death sentences of 167 prisoners and pardoned 4 more.
  • 1787年 – 英国天文学家威廉·赫歇尔借由望远镜首次观测到天卫三和天卫四2颗天王星卫星。
  • 1879年 – 第二代切姆斯福德男爵领导的大英帝国军队入侵祖鲁王国，祖鲁战争爆发。
  • 1922年 – 加拿大多伦多全科医院首次为第1型糖尿病患者注射动物胰岛素治疗，获得临床上的成功。
  • 1923年 – 由于魏玛共和国未能履行战后赔款，法国和比利时军队进驻鲁尔区实施军事占领。
  • 1935年 – 美国飞行员阿梅莉亚·埃尔哈特成为首位独自从夏威夷飞到加利福尼亚州的飞行员。

Births and Deaths: Min Bin (d. 1554); Socrates Nelson (b. 1814); Eva Le Gallienne (b. 1899); Eva Tanguay (d. 1947)
