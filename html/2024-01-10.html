On This Day 以史为鉴

January 10
1月10日：圣佳德庆节（天主教会）、中国人民警察节（中华人民共和国）

  • 236 – Pope Fabian, said to have been chosen by the Holy Spirit when a dove landed on his head, began his papacy.
  • 1812 – New Orleans, the first steamship on the Mississippi River, arrived in its namesake city to complete its maiden voyage.
  • 1929 – Tintin in the Land of the Soviets, the first volume of The Adventures of Tintin by the Belgian cartoonist Hergé, began serialisation.
  • 1993 – The Braer Storm, the strongest extratropical cyclone ever recorded in the North Atlantic, reached its peak intensity.
  • 2004 – Helge Fossmo, the village priest of Knutby, Sweden, orchestrated the murders of his wife and his neighbor, a crime that shocked the country.
  • 前49年 – 儒略·恺撒及其第十三合组军团违反罗马法横渡卢比孔河，恺撒内战爆发。
  • 9年 – 西汉外戚王莽强迫皇帝孺子婴禅让以篡位成为皇帝，并更改国号建立新朝。
  • 1776年 – 美国思想家托马斯·潘恩发表《常识》一书，论述争取十三殖民地全面独立的观点。
  • 1863年 – 英国伦敦地铁的大都会铁路完成帕丁顿车站与法灵顿站之间通车，为世界首条地铁路线。
  • 1946年 – 联合国大会首次会议在英国伦敦卫理公会中央礼堂举行，共有51个会员国代表参与。

Births and Deaths: Georg Forster (d. 1794); Hrithik Roshan (b. 1974); Yip Pin Xiu (b. 1992); Constantine II of Greece (d. 2023)
