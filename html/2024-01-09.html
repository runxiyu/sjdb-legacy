On This Day 以史为鉴

January 9
1月9日：圣斯德望日（东方基督教）、菲律宾黑拿撒勒人节

  • 1797 – War of the First Coalition: The Siege of Kehl, enacted by Habsburg and Württemberg forces, ended when French troops withdrew from their fortifications.
  • 1917 – First World War: Troops of the British Empire defeated Ottoman forces at the Battle of Rafa on the Sinai–Palestine border in present-day Rafah.
  • 1972 – The Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association lost to the Milwaukee Bucks, ending a 33-game winning streak, the longest in major American professional team sports.
  • 1975 – In Central and Southeastern United States, a Great Storm formed the first of forty-five tornados over a three-day period.
  • 1992 – Radio astronomers Aleksander Wolszczan and Dale Frail announced the discovery of two planets orbiting the pulsar PSR B1257+12, generally considered the first definitive detection of an exoplanet.
  • 1127年 – 来自中国北方的金朝军队攻陷宋朝首都汴梁，俘虏宋朝皇帝宋钦宗和太上皇宋徽宗。
  • 1878年 – 意大利国王维托里奥·埃马努埃莱二世逝世，其子翁贝托一世继位成为新任国王。
  • 1916年 – 奥斯曼帝国在加里波利之战中击退英国和法国的攻势，迫使后者从加里波利半岛撤退。
  • 1988年 – 中华民国国家中山科学研究院上校张宪义叛逃至美国，最终导致中华民国政府被迫放弃发展核子武器。
  • 2005年 – 巴勒斯坦解放组织法塔赫候选人马哈茂德·阿巴斯当选巴勒斯坦自治政府主席。

Births and Deaths: T. W. Robertson (b. 1829); Carrie Chapman Catt (b. 1859); Farhan Akhtar (b. 1974); Lei Jieqiong (d. 2011)
