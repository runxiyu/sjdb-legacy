On This Day 以史为鉴

December 22: December solstice (03:28 UTC, 2023); Yule begins; Dongzhi Festival in China (2023)
12月22日：冬至、印度尼西亚母亲节

  • 401 – Pope Innocent I began his papacy, succeeding Anastasius I.
  • 1807 – In an effort to avoid engaging in the Napoleonic Wars, the United States Congress passed the Embargo Act, forbidding American ships from engaging in trade with foreign nations.
  • 1984 – While riding a New York City Subway train, Bernhard Goetz shot four African-American youths who had attempted to rob him, sparking a nationwide debate on vigilantism, racism, and the legal limits of self-defense.
  • 2001 – Richard Reid unsuccessfully attempted to detonate a bomb in his shoe on a transatlantic flight from Paris to Miami.
  • 2008 – A dike ruptured at a waste containment area for a Tennessee Valley Authority coal-fired power plant in Kingston, Tennessee, U.S., releasing 1.1 billion US gallons (4,200,000 m3) of coal fly ash slurry in the largest industrial spill in U.S. history.
  • 1769年 – 清朝军队和缅甸贡榜王朝军队因为战况拖延而同意签署临时和约，结束清缅战争。
  • 1808年 – 德国作曲家路德维希·范·贝多芬创作的《第五交响曲》在维也纳河畔剧院首次演出。
  • 1885年 – 日本正式建立内阁制度，由伊藤博文出任首任内阁总理大臣，组成第一次伊藤内阁。
  • 1974年 – 大科摩罗岛、昂儒昂岛与莫埃利岛在经过公民投票后，决定共同组成独立国家科摩罗。
  • 1978年 – 中共十一届三中全会在邓小平等主要领导人主导下，决定推行改革开放政策。

Births and Deaths: Cynesige (d. 1060); Teresa Carreño (b. 1853); Basshunter (b. 1984)
