On This Day 以史为鉴

January 12: Zanzibar Revolution Day in Tanzania (1964)
1月12日：土库曼斯坦纪念日（1881年）、坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔革命日（1964年）

  • 1659 – The fort at Allahabad was surrendered to the forces of Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb.
  • 1879 – Anglo-Zulu War: Natal Native Contingent and British troops defeated Zulu forces in the Action at Sihayo's Kraal and burned down the neighbouring settlement.
  • 1899 – During a storm, the crew of the Lynmouth Lifeboat Station transported their 10-ton lifeboat 15 mi (24 km) overland in order to rescue a damaged schooner.
  • 1969 – British rock band Led Zeppelin released their eponymous first album in the United States.
  • 2007 – Comet McNaught reached perihelion, becoming the brightest comet in over 40 years, with an apparent magnitude of −5.5.
  • 1872年 – 约翰尼斯四世在阿克苏姆加冕为埃塞俄比亚帝国皇帝，也是该城市200年来首次举行加冕典礼。
  • 1945年 – 苏联红军向纳粹德国发动大规模攻势，成功将战线由维斯瓦河推进至奥得河。
  • 1959年 – 致力于推广“汽车城”黑人灵魂乐风格音乐的摩城唱片公司在美国底特律成立。
  • 1964年 – 桑给巴尔非洲-设拉子党非洲裔成员发动革命推翻桑给巴尔苏丹国政府，并成立人民共和国。
  • 2010年 – 海地首都太子港附近发生ML 7.0级的强烈地震，造成超过100,000人死亡。

Births and Deaths: Godscall Paleologue (b. 1694); Laura Adams Armer (b. 1874); Princess Patricia of Connaught (d. 1974); Olga Ladyzhenskaya (d. 2004)
