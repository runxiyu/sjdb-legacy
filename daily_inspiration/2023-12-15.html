Sonnet
Beigou

Shall I compare thee to a bagel's grace?
Thou art more tempting in thy circular space:
With golden crust and tender, chewy core,
Oh bagel, thou art a delight to adore!

The aroma of baking fills the air,
With cream cheese or jam, beyond compare.
Morning's beginning, lunchtime's delight, In your presence,
the world feels just right.

In New York or Montreal, you're renowned,
Countless flavors and shapes to be found.
Single or double, it matters not at all,
You're the crown jewel of breakfast, standing tall.

Whether whole wheat or plain,
you shine so bright, Your charm can't be dimmed, your taste takes flight.
You'll never wither, you'll never grow old,
Your delicious legacy, forever told.

十四行诗
贝狗

我是否可以把你比喻成贝果？
就如早晨的阳光一般明媚：
金黄的外皮，内部柔软松脆，
贝果啊，你是美味的艺术品！

烘烤的香气填满了整个厨房，
搭配奶酪或果酱，总是美味；
早晨的开始，午餐的享受，
你是一天中最美妙的时刻。

在纽约或蒙特利尔，你流行无边，
各种口味和形状，不计其数；
无论是一层或两层，你都令人陶醉，
你是早餐的皇冠，无人可及。

无论是全麦还是百吉，无所谓，
你的魅力无法被忽视，无法阻挡；
永远不会褪去，永远不会凋零，
你的美味将永远在人们的唇上流传。
