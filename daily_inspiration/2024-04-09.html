Global warming is heating ocean surface temperatures to record levels.

Deadly tropical species have spread to parts of the Mediterranean, such as the Adriatic Sea, wiping out native species and damaging the ecosystem.

These species, such as the parrot fish, have no value to local residents.

This situation creates uncertainty about the future of the fishing industry.
